Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio! Presented here are the fruits of my labor across my journey in front-end engineering and design. The four projects I present here visibly highlight the values I learned were important when designing interfaces for users. Starting my journey with a simple Hi-Fi prototype Studio utilizing Figma, and ending with a fully functional site that organizes colleges by various criteria, I have learned the wide variety of purposes and forms front-end engineering can take. At each step, I found myself loving and enjoying this process as my creativity was fully allowed to come to the forefront in a way like never before. With new principles of design becoming apparent to me as I progressed, I now feel confident in my ability to design sites that not only look good, but are functional and accessible for all! With each of these projects, I now want to dedicate myself to becoming a UI UX designer committed to turning complicated programs into simple and accessible designs that everyone can navigate! Please enjoy!
